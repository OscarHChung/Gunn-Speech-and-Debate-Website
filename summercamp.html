Gunn S&D Summer Camp

Who Are We?

Gunn Speech and Debate is an entirely student run award-winning club that prides itself on its ability to teach novices and get debaters to the highest levels of excellence. We focus on motivation and strive toward a warm, open community that can give back during times of need such as this one. That is why we are hosting an online summer camp, where rising 6th to 9th graders, regardless of their experience can spend a week understanding more about public speaking and argumentation!
